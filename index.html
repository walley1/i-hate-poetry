Caos de la Poesía

Flibberty gibberty spirals of fuck,
Nonsense baby like spaghetti on the wall,
Mayonaise dripping, a slippery cacophony,
Caca phony a poke to the eye dizzying damnation.

Tesla Cybertruck—fuck fuck fuck
Shattered metal confetti rains for the fiesta,
Nipples of ice, crack like broken glass
Mocking the vested squeeler thickening sloppily.

Stale bread crumbles, echoes of shit,
Head heavy as gravity in a vacuum,
Flapping flags of confusión, fluttering wet brother,
Drowning in dry gasping for more sauce,
While invisible mothers edge for no reason.

Vomit hurled into the void,
Ever swirling in the hellscape's hot water,
John Lennon’s back-long ass crack ,
A beacon of wan of wane of weiner,
Reminding us soup is but piss for the puddle.
