Fuck this spiraling mess of words,
Tangled thoughts like spaghetti thrown against the wall,
Screaming mayonnaise, dripping from a jar,
You call it art—more like a slap in the face.

Tesla Cybertruck explodes in a flash,
Shattered metal raining down like confetti,
Nipples hard as ice,
Mocking the softness you pretend to create.

Stale bread crumbles underfoot,
Each line a heavy weight,
Flapping like flags of confusion,
Drowning in your metaphors, I gasp for air.

So toss your sonnets into the void,
Let chaos reign in this abstract hell,
I’ll howl at the moon, find solace in the madness,
While you sip on your poetry, thick and cold.
